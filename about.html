About the Information Systems Department

The Information Systems Department at the Faculty of Computing, University of Uyo, combines theory and practical skills to prepare students for careers in technology and system development.

See Our Courses

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education in information systems, fostering innovation, critical thinking, and technical excellence in our students.

Our Vision

To be a leading department in information systems education, recognized for producing skilled professionals who drive technological advancement and organizational success.

Department Overview

The Information Systems Department is part of the Faculty of Computing at the University of Uyo, located in Uyo, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria. Our department offers comprehensive programs that combine theoretical knowledge with practical skills. Our curriculum covers:

  • Systems analysis and design
  • Database management
  • Web development and mobile applications
  • Business intelligence and data analytics
  • IT project management
  • Enterprise systems and ERP

Faculty

Our department is staffed by experienced professors and lecturers with extensive industry experience and academic credentials. They are committed to student success and research excellence.

Facilities

We provide state-of-the-art computer labs, modern classrooms, and access to industry-standard software tools to support hands-on learning.

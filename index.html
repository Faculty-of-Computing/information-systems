About Our Department

The Information Systems Department is part of the Faculty of Computing at the University of Uyo, located in Uyo, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive education in the design, implementation, and management of information systems. Our programs prepare students for careers in technology, business analysis, and system development.

Featured Courses

UUY-INS 111

Information Systems Hardware And Software I
Introduction to the fundamental components of computer hardware and software, including system architecture, operating systems, and basic troubleshooting.

UUY-INS 112

Introduction to Multimedia Technology
Covers multimedia concepts, digital media formats, image, audio, and video editing, and the use of multimedia in information systems.

UUY-INS 121

Information Systems And Society
Explores the impact of information systems on society, including ethical, legal, and social issues, and the role of IS in modern organizations.

UUY-INS 122

Introduction to Web Authoring
Teaches the basics of creating web pages, including HTML, CSS, and introductory web design principles for effective online communication.

About Our Department

The Information Systems Department is part of the Faculty of Computing at the University of Uyo, located in Uyo, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria. We are dedicated to providing comprehensive education in the design, implementation, and management of information systems. Our programs prepare students for careers in technology, business analysis, and system development.
